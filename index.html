About Me

My introduction
Download CV

I am a PhD candidate at Music Technology Group from Universitat Pompeu Fabra in collaboration with BMAT Licensing SL under the supervision of Professor Xavier Serra and Dr. Alex Ciurana.

I'm currently studying audio identification technologies and how to increase its robustness in noisy environments. Even though now I'm focused on Music Information Retrieval tasks, my research interests go beyond that, as my previous works show.

Before going into the world of Music Information Retrieval, I researched on multiple areas like biometrics, speech, machine translation. At University of Edinburgh, I worked with Barry Haddow and Marta Ruiz Costa-Jussà on studying the robustness of speech translation systems. At Telefónica I+D, in a team lead by Jordi Luque, we developed a prototype of a biometric authentication system based on optical pulse signal.
